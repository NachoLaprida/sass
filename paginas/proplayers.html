The Viper

TheViper is known for his late game capabilities, decision making, exceptional macro and unit control. Becoming an active player in 2010, TheViper has won more tournaments than any other AoE2 player in history and is often considered to be the best AoE2 player of all time.

Liereyy

One of the younger members of the in the scene, who is best known for his ability to intensively micromanage his units without missing a beat in his economy.

Daut

DauT has been playing competitive Age of Empires 2 since 2002, taking few breaks in the past 19 years Despite being a bit slower than some of the younger players, he more than makes up for it with his superb macro and creative strategies.

Hera

Known as one of the fastest players in the scene, he used to use an extremely aggressive micro-oriented style, but his rise to the top coincided with a change in style which has led to him being regarded as the best defensive player in the game. .

JorDan

During TheViper's rise to prominence, JorDan stood as his greatest rival; they traded sets back and forth against each other, eventually culminating in a set of 101 consecutive games against each other in 2015, which TheViper won 53-48.

Mr_Yo

Considered the best Chinese Age of Empires 2 player for years and known for his masterful planning, attentive map control, and excellent late-game awareness, Yo's style of aggressive play brings to mind a surgeon, and he particularly excels in non-standard settings.
